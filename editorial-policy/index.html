Editorial Policy

This page explains how content on Rajib.uk is created, reviewed, and updated.

Authorship

Rajib.uk is a personal site. Unless otherwise stated, articles, tutorials, and explanatory pages are written or directly reviewed by Muhammad Rajib Hawlader.

Content goals

The goal of the site is to publish useful, experience-based content about software engineering, project delivery, architecture, security-minded development, and related professional work. The site aims to prioritize clarity and relevance over high-volume publishing.

Accuracy and updates

Content is reviewed and updated when practical, especially where technical context changes or a page would otherwise become misleading. Some content represents perspective or experience at the time of writing and may not reflect every possible implementation scenario.

Originality

Rajib.uk aims to publish original summaries, project narratives, and commentary based on direct knowledge, engineering practice, or personal authorship. The site does not aim to mass-produce thin content for search or advertising alone.

Advertising independence

If display advertising is used on the site in the future, editorial content will continue to be selected for usefulness and relevance rather than solely for ad impressions. Advertising will not determine the factual or professional opinions expressed in tutorials or insight articles.

Useful content should remain useful even without advertising.
