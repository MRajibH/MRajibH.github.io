Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Rajib.uk may collect, use, and protect information when visitors browse the site, contact the site owner, or interact with linked services.

Information collected

Rajib.uk may collect information that visitors voluntarily provide through contact forms or direct email, such as name, email address, organization, and message content. The site may also collect limited technical data through hosting, security, and analytics tools, including IP address, browser type, device information, referral source, and page usage activity.

How information may be used

  • To respond to enquiries, collaboration requests, or professional contact.
  • To maintain site security, prevent abuse, and diagnose technical issues.
  • To understand which pages are useful and improve the content over time.
  • To comply with legal obligations when required.

Cookies and analytics

Rajib.uk may use basic analytics or hosting diagnostics to understand traffic and site performance. These tools may rely on cookies or similar browser storage depending on the hosting or analytics setup in use at the time. If advertising products such as Google AdSense are enabled in the future, additional cookies or ad-related data handling may apply and this policy may be updated accordingly.

Third-party links

The site links to third-party services such as LinkedIn, GitHub, ResearchGate, X, and Facebook. Once a visitor leaves Rajib.uk and visits those services, their own privacy practices will apply.

Data retention and security

Contact submissions or direct communications may be retained for professional follow-up, record keeping, or abuse prevention for as long as reasonably necessary. Reasonable technical and organizational measures may be used to protect information, but no internet transmission or storage system is completely guaranteed secure.

Your choices

Visitors who do not wish to provide personal information should avoid submitting forms or sending direct email. If you have submitted personal information and want it updated or removed where reasonably possible, you may use the contact details provided on the site.

Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in site features, legal expectations, or analytics and advertising tools. Continued use of the site after a material update indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about privacy can be sent through the site contact details.
